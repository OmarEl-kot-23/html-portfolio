The Best Movies According to Omar

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Jobs (2013)

This biographical drama film depicts the life of Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple Inc. It showcases his journey from college dropout to tech visionary.

The Social Network (2010)

This film tells the story of the creation of Facebook and the legal battles that followed. It highlights the complexities of friendship, ambition, and innovation in the digital age.

Pulp Fiction (1994)

Directed by Quentin Tarantino, this cult classic weaves together multiple interconnected stories of crime and redemption in Los Angeles, known for its sharp dialogue and memorable characters.
